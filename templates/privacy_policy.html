Welcome to GoogleDiscoverChecker.com

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit GoogleDiscoverChecker.com (the "Website") and use our Google Discover Checker Analyzer service.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide:

2. Automatically Collected Information:

We use the collected information to:

  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends

Data Security

At GoogleDiscoverChecker.com, we implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Payment processing (if applicable)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data

Updates to This Policy
